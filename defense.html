The challenge

Every device, a different battery

Field units carry a zoo of incompatible packs, chargers, and connectors. Weight goes up, interoperability goes down, and resupply becomes its own mission. A common integration layer changes that math.

Ruggedized by requirement

Vibration, shock, humidity, and wide-temperature operation treated as design inputs, not afterthoughts.

One platform, many devices

Standardized packs and interfaces across radios, sensors, and unmanned systems simplify training and resupply.

Readiness you can query

State-of-health telemetry turns "is this pack good?" from a guess into a data point before the mission, not during it.
