What we do

Freeing products from fixed battery constraints

Most products are designed around whatever battery format was available at the time — and stay locked to it. Auravolt reverses that relationship.

From

One chemistry, one format, one ceiling

Off-the-shelf packs force compromises on runtime, weight, charge speed, and safety margins. Switching cells later means redesign, requalification, and months of lost time.

To

Integration built around your requirements

We engineer the pack, battery management, thermal design, and product interface as one system — so you can adopt the best cell for the job today, and swap in better chemistry tomorrow.

How we do it

The Auravolt Integration Core

A modular hardware-and-software layer that sits between cells and product — handling monitoring, protection, balancing, and communication so your device just sees clean, reliable power.

Cell-agnostic by design

Work with lithium-ion, LFP, sodium-ion, or emerging chemistries without re-architecting your product.

Optimized performance

Precision state-of-charge and state-of-health tracking gets more usable energy out of every pack, safely.

Seamless integration

Standard electrical and data interfaces cut integration time from months to weeks — no recertification spiral.

AI-ready architecture

Every pack streams telemetry from day one — the foundation for the predictive intelligence on our roadmap.

Where we're headed

Batteries first. Intelligence next.

Auravolt is building in deliberate phases: earn trust with hardware that ships, then leverage the data layer that hardware creates.

Now Phase 01

Custom battery integration

Engineering services and integration hardware for product manufacturers who need power designed around their device.

  • Pack design, BMS, and thermal engineering
  • Prototyping through production support
  • Certification and safety guidance
Next Phase 02

AI-driven energy intelligence

Software built on live fleet telemetry from deployed Auravolt systems — turning battery data into operating advantage.

  • Predictive health and failure forecasting
  • Adaptive charging and load optimization
  • Fleet analytics and warranty intelligence
