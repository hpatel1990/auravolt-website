The problem

Why battery upgrades stall

Locked to a legacy pack

The battery decision was made years ago, and every subsystem now assumes it — voltage rails, mounting, charging, firmware.

Redesign risk

Changing cells means requalifying the whole product: new safety testing, new tooling, new failure modes, new schedule slips.

Supply fragility

A single-sourced pack is a single point of failure. One allocation crunch or discontinuation can freeze your production line.
