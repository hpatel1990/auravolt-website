Why Auravolt exists

Battery innovation is moving faster than products can absorb it. We started Auravolt to close that gap — first with integration engineering, then with the intelligence layer that engineering makes possible.

Our story

Founded on a simple observation

Every year brings genuinely better cells — safer chemistries, longer cycle lives, lower costs. And every year, most products keep shipping with the same packs they launched with, because the integration cost of switching is too high.

Auravolt was founded in 2026 to attack that integration cost directly. Our mark tells the story: two halves — power and product — engineered to meet in the middle.

Values

How we work

Safety before speed

Batteries fail loudly. We'd rather lose a week to a design review than lose a customer to a field incident.

Engineering honesty

Real numbers, stated error bars, and a straight "no" when a chemistry doesn't fit the application.

Data compounds

Every deployed pack teaches the platform something. We build so that today's telemetry becomes tomorrow's intelligence.
