Become a cell partner
The gap

Between a great cell and a shipped product

Winning an OEM design slot usually means months of custom pack engineering, application-specific BMS work, and certification support — for every single customer. That doesn't scale, and it keeps promising chemistries stuck in pilot purgatory.

The Auravolt Integration Core absorbs that work once. Characterize your cell against our platform, and every Auravolt-enabled product becomes an addressable socket for your chemistry.

Partner program

What cell partnership includes

Cell characterization

We profile your chemistry against the Integration Core and build the cell model our firmware uses.

Reference designs

Validated pack configurations your sales team can hand to prospects as a working starting point.

Route to OEMs

Placement in front of manufacturers actively scoping projects that match your cell's strengths.

Co-marketing

Joint case studies and launch material once your chemistry is running in a shipping product.

Good fits

Chemistries we're actively looking for

Sodium-ion

Cost- and safety-advantaged cells looking for stationary backup and commercial applications where energy density is negotiable.

LFP & LTO

Long-cycle-life chemistries for critical power and industrial duty cycles that punish conventional packs.

Emerging formats

Novel form factors and next-generation chemistries that need a credible integration story to win first design slots.
